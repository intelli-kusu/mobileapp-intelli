Welcome to the virtual care service provided by your primary care clinic. This added service allows you to get access to a team of providers that you talk to via an audio or video session for all urgent and immediate general sickness, questions regarding your health, or prescription refill. It comes at no additional cost to you, and you may continue to receive care from the primary care doctor. You may access the service through this app anytime to get into a virtual waiting room. Providers will return your call from 6:00 AM to 10 PM Pacific Time, all days of the week except Wednesdays. 

 

Please enter the information below to continue. You will receive a one-time password via text message on the mobile number provided. Once verified:

  • If you or a dependent is using the service the first time, the app will guide you through the enrolment screen before creating a visit from the dashboard.
  • Repeat patients can create a visit from the dashboard.
